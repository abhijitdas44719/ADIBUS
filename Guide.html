1. Getting started

Create an account to save passengers, get offers, and manage bookings. You can also continue as a guest for quick bookings.

2. Search routes

Use the search bar on the homepage to enter origin, destination and travel date. Add return date for round trips.

  1. Select origin and destination cities. Use the suggestions or enter city/station names.
  2. Choose departure date (and return date if needed).
  3. Tap Search to see available buses and timings.

Filters and sort

Narrow results by:

  • Departure time (morning/afternoon/night)
  • Bus type (AC/Non-AC/Sleeper/Seater)
  • Operator rating, fare range, amenities (wifi, charging)

3. Select a bus & seat

After choosing a bus, select preferred seats from the seat map. Prices may vary by seat type (lower/upper, window/aisle).

1

4. Payment

We accept debit/credit cards, net banking, UPI and popular wallets. Payment is secured using industry-standard encryption.

Steps to pay

  1. Review fare breakdown: base fare, taxes, convenience fee.
  2. Apply coupon codes at checkout (if any).
  3. Select payment method and complete payment.

  • Amount debited but booking not confirmed ‚Äî contact support with transaction reference.

7. Offers & Coupons

Apply coupon codes during checkout. Only one coupon may be applied per booking unless stated otherwise. Coupons have expiry and eligibility criteria.

8. FAQ

What IDs are accepted at boarding?

Government-issued photo IDs: Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License; some operators accept college or company ID with photo.

Can I change passenger name?

Name changes are not allowed in most cases. Contact support for operator-specific exceptions.

How long is my ticket valid?

Ticket is valid for the scheduled journey date and time only.
