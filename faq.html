Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bus ticket?
Go to our homepage, select your route and date, and follow the on-screen instructions to book your ticket.
Can I cancel or reschedule my ticket?
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule by logging into your account and accessing 'My Bookings'. Cancellation fees may apply.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.
Will I get a refund if I cancel the ticket?
Refunds depend on the cancellation policy of the bus operator. The refund amount will be shown before you confirm cancellation.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us via our 24/7 chat support or call our helpline at 1800-123-4567.
