Safety Guidelines

Your safety and the safety of our drivers is our top priority. Please review these guidelines to ensure a safe journey for everyone.

üöè

At the Bus Stop

  • Arrive at least 5 minutes before the scheduled departure time
  • Wait on the sidewalk, well back from the curb
  • Wear light-colored or reflective clothing at night
  • Never cross the street directly in front of or behind a stopped bus
  • Keep personal belongings close to you
üöå

Boarding the Bus

  • Allow passengers to exit before you board
  • Have your fare, pass, or ticket ready before boarding
  • Use handrails when climbing steps
  • Secure large items like strollers or carts
  • Move toward the rear of the bus to make room for others
üí∫

While Onboard

  • Remain seated whenever possible
  • Wear seatbelts if available
  • Keep aisles and exits clear at all times
  • Hold handrails firmly if standing
  • Be courteous to fellow passengers and the driver
  • Use headphones for audio devices
üö™

Exiting the Bus

  • Signal your stop in advance by pulling the cord or pressing the button
  • Remain seated until the bus comes to a complete stop
  • Check for personal belongings before exiting
  • Use handrails when going down the steps
  • Wait for the bus to pull away before crossing the street
  • Cross at designated crosswalks only
üö´

Prohibited Items & Behaviors

  • Smoking, vaping, or tobacco use of any kind
  • Alcohol or illegal substances
  • Flammable, explosive, or hazardous materials
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Unsecured animals (service animals welcome)
  • Disruptive or abusive behavior

Special Assistance

  • Priority seating for seniors and passengers with disabilities
  • Wheelchair ramps and securement areas available
  • Stop announcements for visually impaired passengers
  • Ask the driver for assistance if needed
  • Designated areas for strollers and mobility devices
